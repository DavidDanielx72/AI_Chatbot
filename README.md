The Basic Rights & Social Grant (SASSA) Navigator is an AI-powered conversational assistant designed to make essential government services and basic legal information more accessible to South Africans. The chatbot helps users understand eligibility requirements, required documentation, and application processes for services such as SASSA grants, identity document applications, and basic labour rights. By providing clear, easy-to-understand guidance, the chatbot aims to reduce unnecessary travel, long queues, and confusion while empowering citizens to access the support and services they are entitled to.
